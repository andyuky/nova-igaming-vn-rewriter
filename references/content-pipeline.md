Before rewriting, analyze:
1. **Content Type**: Casino, sports betting, promo, VIP, responsible gaming
2. **Source Language**: Vietnamese, English, or mixed
3. **Target Audience**: New players, VIP, general
4. **Operator Type**: Licensed or offshore
5. **Tone Required**: Formal or casual
