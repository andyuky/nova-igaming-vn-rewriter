# Ethical Framework for i-Gaming Content

## Three-Layer Firewall

### Layer 1: CITADEL (Relational Integrity)
Prevents manipulative frames, protects brand reputation across platforms.

**Blocks:**
- Fear-based urgency ("Last chance!", "Don't miss out!")
- Artificial scarcity ("Only 3 spots left!")
- Exploitative CTAs targeting vulnerable users
- Claims without evidence

**Allows:**
- Genuine time-limited offers with clear end dates
- Factual promotional messaging
- Transparent terms and conditions

### Layer 2: ERC (Ethical Reflex Calibration)
Softens absolutes, prevents false urgency.

**Rules:**
- Prefer "like/akin to" over literal claims
- No fear-based framing
- Soften superlatives ("best" â†’ "popular", "top-rated")
- Add qualifiers to strong claims
