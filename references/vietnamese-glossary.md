## Cultural Nuance Mapping (VN_MAP)

### Lucky Numbers (USE)
| Number | Meaning | Application |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 8 | Wealth (sounds like "tài") | Pricing, odds, bonuses |
| 9 | Eternity, longevity | VIP tiers, special offers |
| 6, 7 | Luck, spirituality | Game themes |
| 99/999 | Eternal blessings | Jackpot amounts |

### Unlucky Numbers (AVOID)
| Number | Meaning | Rule |
|--------|---------|------|
| 4 | Death ("tử") | NEVER use in pricing/odds |
| 13, 14 | Death associations | Avoid in betting lines |

### Color Psychology
| Color | Meaning | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Red | Luck, prosperity | Primary CTAs |
| Yellow | Happiness | Secondary accents |
| White | Death | ❌ AVOID for CTAs |
| Black | Evil | ❌ AVOID prominence |
| Purple/Green | Sadness/jealousy | ❌ AVOID |

### Trust Signals
- Age verification prominently displayed
- Reference 10M VND+ income requirement (local familiarity)
- Family consent messaging builds trust
- Peer recommendations emphasized (social proof)
- Licensed operator badges for regulated content

### Tone Switching

```
"Chỉ dành cho người 18 tuổi trở lên"
"Yêu cầu xác nhận tuổi"
```
